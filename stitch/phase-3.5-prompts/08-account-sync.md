# 08 Account + Sync

Use `00-shared-brief.md` as the base direction.

Create the mobile account and sync surface for `MyoRep Timer`.

## Goal
- Make account identity and sync state understandable on mobile without turning this into the main feature of the app.
- Support the states introduced in Phases 2 and 3 cleanly.

## Must Show
- Signed-in Plus state.
- Signed-in free state.
- Syncing or last-synced state.
- Offline or sync-error state.
- A clear place for magic-link sign-in or sign-out actions.

## Intent
- The screen should communicate trust, clarity, and control.
- Sync should feel secondary to the timer workflow, but still understandable and actionable.

## Notes
- Keep guest, free, and Plus distinctions visually legible.
- Do not overcomplicate the surface with billing-system complexity; focus on identity and sync status.
